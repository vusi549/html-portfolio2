About Me

I am a dedicated and versatile educator with a strong academic background in Computer Science, Electronics,
and Education, supported by professional training in TEFL and a Postgraduate Certificate in Education (PGCE).
My passion lies in creating engaging, learner-centered environments where students can build confidence,
develop critical thinking skills, and master new concepts in both English and technology.

With formal qualifications ranging from Computer Science (Bachelor’s degree) to Honors-level study,
I bring a rich understanding of computational thinking, problem-solving, and digital literacy.
This technical foundation allows me to integrate modern, innovative teaching methods into my lessons,
especially in areas such as coding, robotics, and digital skills.

My PGCE training has equipped me with solid pedagogical strategies, classroom-management skills,
and curriculum design experience, enabling me to support diverse learning needs while maintaining
high academic standards. Combined with my TEFL certification, I am able to adapt my teaching approach
for English language learners, helping them improve fluency, communication, and confidence in a
supportive environment.

I am committed to continuous growth, professionalism, and fostering a safe, respectful, and inspiring
learning atmosphere. Whether working with children, teens, or adults, I strive to empower every learner
and help them unlock their full potential—academically and personally.
